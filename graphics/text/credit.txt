LibTech1 is a fork of Freedoom, a complete
set of assets for the Doom engine that is
wholly free and open-source.

Thanks go to all the different artists,
musicians, level designers and programmers
who have contributed to Freedoom to allow
this and other forks to happen.

As with Freedoom, LibTech1 is distributed
under the terms of the BSD license.

For more information, check out:

    https://libtech1.nfshost.com/
    https://freedoom.github.io/
